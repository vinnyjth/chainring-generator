Handlebar / Clamp Diameter Gauge

Generate a 3D-printable saddle gauge to identify the clamp diameter of a handlebar, stem, or seat collar. Each labelled notch cradles one diameter — lay the round tube in the notches; the one it nests into without rocking is its size.
