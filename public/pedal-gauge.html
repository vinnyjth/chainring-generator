Pedal Thread Gauge

Generate a 3D-printable go / no-go card to tell a 9⁄16″ pedal (standard adult cranks) from a 1⁄2″ pedal (one-piece / BMX / kids cranks). Push the pedal spindle through the holes — the smallest it passes is its thread size.

Card
Printer

Default is the Bambu Lab P1S (256 mm bed) with a small safety margin.

Both common pedal threads are 20 TPI — only the diameter differs, so a plain hole tells them apart. Left pedals are reverse-threaded but the same diameter. The rare French 14×1.25 mm thread is too close to 9⁄16″ to separate here. Verify with calipers.
