Seatpost Size Gauge

Generate a 3D-printable gauge to identify a seatpost / seat-tube diameter. Choose a stacked bike tower or a flat hole card — each size is printed on the part. Tuned for the Bambu Lab P1S build volume.

Tower: each ring's outer ⌀ is its labelled size — insert the small end into the seat tube; the largest ring that slides in is your size. Splits into telescoping segments if taller than the printer.
