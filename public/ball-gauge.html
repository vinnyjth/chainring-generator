Ball-Bearing Size Gauge

Generate a 3D-printable sieve card to sort loose bicycle ball bearings. Drop a ball into the holes; the smallest one it drops through is its size. Labels are in fractional inches (the way loose balls are sold).

Sizes

Card
Printer

Default is the Bambu Lab P1S (256 mm bed) with a small safety margin.

Common bike ball sizes: 1/8″ (rear hubs), 5/32″ (front hubs), 3/16″ (headsets / BB), 7/32″ and 1/4″ (BB / headsets). Tune the clearance and verify against a known ball with calipers before trusting the gauge.
